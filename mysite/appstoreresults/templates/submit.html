Submit an App to Code V. Wade

We want Code v. Wade to be a comprehensive resource of every reproductive health application out there! If you are curious about an app that we haven't yet investigated, follow these steps to help us provide valuable privacy information to the people!

  • Go to the Google Play Store
  • Use the search bar to find the app

  • Right click the app and select "Copy link"

  • Paste the link and click "Submit"

  • In order to conduct an in-depth investigation into the privacy of reproductive health apps, our analysis can take some time to complete.
  • Some parts of our analysis can be done immediately, such as gathering information on the permissions that an app requests.
  • Other parts involve manual analysis by a member of our team, such as determining if data is encrypted in transit.
  • Submitting an app triggers the automatic analysis of the app and notifies the Code v. Wade team that some manual analysis is required.
  • No information about you will be sent to the team or stored other than the link to the app that was submitted
